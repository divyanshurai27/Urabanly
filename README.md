URBANLY — Smart Business Location & Idea Analyzer

Stop guessing. Start building where it actually works.

🚀 Overview

Urbanly is an AI-powered platform that helps users make smarter business decisions based on location intelligence and market demand.

Instead of relying on assumptions, Urbanly analyzes factors like crowd patterns, demand trends, and local competition to answer two critical questions:

📍 “I have a location — what should I sell?”
📦 “I have a product — where should I open?”
⚡ Problem Statement

Most people:

Open businesses blindly
Choose locations based on gut feeling
Ignore real data like demand, competition, and crowd trends

Result?
👉 Businesses fail before they even get traction.

💡 Solution

Urbanly bridges this gap using AI + data analysis to provide:

📊 Location scoring
📈 Demand insights
🏪 Business suggestions
📍 Ideal location recommendations
